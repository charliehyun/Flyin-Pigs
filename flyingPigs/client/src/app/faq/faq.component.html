Frequently Asked Questions

Flyin' Pigs was created by Charlie Hyun, Mark, Sarah Mi, and Jessica Yeh for their 2022 Senior Design Project (CS 407) at Purdue University. It was created to help users plan trips more efficiently with the ability to start and end their trips from local addresses to check out more airports. TheAPIwe use to get the flight data does not support American Airlines at this time. Southwest doesn't let third party apps like Google Flights, Expedia, Kayak or other travel agencies(including us :p) share or sell its fares. We use
  • Google Maps APIfor driving and transit times
  • Amadeus APIfor flight data
