Flyin' Pigs was created by Charlie Hyun, Mark, Sarah Mi, and Jessica Yeh for their 2022 Senior Design Project (CS 407) at Purdue University. It was created to help users plan trips more efficiently with the ability to start and end their trips from local addresses to check out more airports. If you are just browsing prices for a future trip, click on the Explore Tab to get started. If you have a specific trip in mind, use the Search Trips tab to search for trips.
